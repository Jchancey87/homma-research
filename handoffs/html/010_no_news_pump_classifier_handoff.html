1. What Was Built

A two-phase catalyst classification system that automatically tags every live gainer with one of three tiers and surfaces the result in the UI — so you can instantly tell whether a move is fundamental or pure tape-speed speculation.

Catalyst Tier Taxonomy

TagTrigger ConditionUI
Confirmed Catalystnews_headline is populatedExisting italic headline text in drawer
Technical / No NewsNo news + gap_pct ≥ 30% + rvol_15m ≥ 2xOrange ⚠️ NNP pill in table row + orange badge in drawer
SpeculativeNo news + low or unknown RVOLGray ? SPEC pill in table row + gray badge in drawer
